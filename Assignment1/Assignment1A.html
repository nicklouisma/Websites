Tropical Fruit

Tropical Fruit is a organization that promotes healthy eating, through veganism. We prioritize ourself in consuming fruits, vegetables and whole grains.
We sell fruit by the basket to locations in the California area. Each basket can carry 30 ounces of fruit for $14.99! We want to change this world one fruit at a time, through our healthy fruit options:

  • Bananas
  • Pineapples
  • Guayaba

On Earth Day, April 22nd, we'll be having a sale buy two baskets of fruit and get the third one free!

We're located in San Diego, California. tropicalfruit.pace.edu
